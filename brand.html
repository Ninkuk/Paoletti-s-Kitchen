What is this page? How can you use it?

We have a few guidelines for using our brand resources so please take a moment to familiarize yourself with them. You can download individual assets in each section. If you want everything, use the button below to download a .zip file.

Logo

Our logo is crucial to our business as it is our main branding source. By making it red, the logo would come out in space; thus making our logo more noticeable. Furthermore, the chef hat is essential in the design of the logo, for it signifies that the company cooks their own food and is not a meal delivery service such as ubereats.

With that said, you are free to use our logo, on the condition that the color and design are unchanged. In addition, it is imperative to give space to the logo, and not to encapsulate it in another shape, such as a rectangle, or a square. For in doing so would eliminate the simplicity given to the logo.

Colors

We put a great deal of time into choosing our color scheme for our website. We chose these colors below in order to convey a certain feeling from each one. Red evokes a sense of hunger. The Greens we choose convey fresh and clean food that we guarantee to our customers. For our color accents, we included white and black. The white space on our site shows off clean and simplistic elements and lets the main content of our site grow and we used black to further accentuate those other colors.

Wordmark

Our wordmark is our most simplistic design. We made it in white and black. We used Google Fonts to determine what fonts we were going to use for our body copy and our wordmark. After some decision, we decided to pick our main font Montserrat and the Roboto as our alternate font.
